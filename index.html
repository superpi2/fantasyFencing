TEAM 2059-901

2059-012, 2059-016, 2059-020

CONTROLS

  • Move: Use the left and right arrow keys. Each key press makes the player advance or retreat once.
  • Attack: Use space to attack. Hitting your opponent successfully (touching) gives you one point. You won't be able to touch your opponent if you're standing too close to them.
  • Parry: Use the up or down arrows to parry. Parrying blocks attacks from opponents, and prevents them from attacking again for a second.
  • Menu Navigation: Use the mouse to navigate the menus.

RULES

  • Each match contains three rounds of three minutes each. The match ends after a player reaches 15 points three rounds pass.
  • You can score a point by touching your opponent or if your opponent moves off the edge of the playing area.
  • After beating a certain character, you can play as them. Certain characters appear only in certain stages.
  • Good luck!
